Understand which factors affect the climate visibility on airports

Climate visibility is affected by various factors such as:
1. Dry bulb temperature
2. Wet bulb temperature
3. Relative humidity
4. Wind speed, wind direction and many other parameters.

About

We have use state-of-the-art machine learning technologies to help find the visibility of the climate using parameters such as Dry bulb temperature, Wet bulb temperature, Relative humidity etc. which helps the airport traffic unit to make informed decisions for flight take offs, landings and air traffic management to avoid any visibility related accidents.

Impact of visibility on the flights

Poor visibility

Poor visibility reduce capacity of airports leading to flight diversions, flight cancellations and extra operating costs.

Good visibility

Good visibility improves the capacity of airports leading to lesser ground delays and least number of accidents.

Accidents

Fog and low visibility are one of the major causes of flight accidents every year.
